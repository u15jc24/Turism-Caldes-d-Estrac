Beaches

Platja dels Tres Micos

Caldes d’Estrac is home to what some might say one of the most stunning beaches on the Maresme coastline. It has golden fine sand and clear blue waters. The beach is well equipped containing lifeguard services, showers, and chiringitos. In the chiringitos, also known as beach bars, you can enjoy tapas and drinks. This beautiful escape is just 40 minutes away from Barcelona allowing visitors who are looking for an escape from the fast-paced Barcelona lifestyle and enjoy a relaxing stay by the beach.

Attractions

Thermal Baths

Experience relaxation at the town's historic thermal baths. They have been renowned for their therapeutic properties since the 19th century. The baths offer various spa treatments and are situated in a calming setting.

Price: €5 per person
Hiking & Nature Walks

Caldes d’Estrac offers various walking trails including ones through Montenegre Natural Park. The Cami de la Ronda is also another path that provides breathtaking views of the Maresme coastline.

Price: Free

Paddleboarding and Kayaking

On the Platja dels Tres Micos visitors can rent paddleboards and kayaks to explore the mesmerizing waters of the Mediterranean coast.

Price: €15
