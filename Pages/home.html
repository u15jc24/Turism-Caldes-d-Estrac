Discover the Beauty of My Hometown: Caldes d'Estrac

Discover the charm and allure of Caldes d'Estrac. This picturesque town is affectionately called Caldetas among locals and is nestled along the Maresme coast of Catalonia, Spain.

Map of Caldes d'Estrac

Caldes d'Estrac is situated approximately 36 kilometers northeast of Barcelona. This makes it an ideal escape from the bustling city life. The town is easily accessible via the C-32 highway and the R1 line of the Rodalies train service, ensuring a smooth journey for visitors.
